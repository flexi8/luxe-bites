Honey Lavender Ice Cream

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2/3 cup honey
  • 1 tbsp dried lavender
  1. Heat milk and lavender. Let steep 10 min, then strain.
  2. Stir in honey and cream. Chill, churn and freeze.

Tiramisu Gelato

  • 2 cups mascarpone
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp espresso
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  1. Blend all ingredients. Chill, churn and freeze.
  2. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.

Cherry Garcia

  • 2 cups cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup chopped cherries
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chunks
  1. Churn base, stir in cherries and chocolate. Freeze.

Lemon Basil Sorbet

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp chopped basil
  1. Make simple syrup, add lemon and basil. Chill and churn.

Coconut Cream Ice Cream

  • 2 cans coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  1. Whisk all ingredients. Chill, churn, freeze.

Blackberry Swirl

  • 2 cups cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup blackberry puree
  1. Churn base, then swirl in blackberry puree before freezing.

Matcha Green Tea Ice Cream

  • 2 cups cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp matcha powder
  1. Whisk all until smooth. Chill, churn and freeze.
