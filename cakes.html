Chocolate Fudge Cake

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp vinegar (white or apple cider)
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and flour an 8-inch round cake pan.
  2. Mix dry ingredients, then add wet ingredients. Stir until smooth.
  3. Pour batter into pan and bake for 30–35 mins. Let cool before serving.

Classic Vanilla Sponge

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1½ cups sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease two 8-inch pans.
  2. Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs one at a time. Mix dry and wet ingredients alternately.
  3. Divide batter and bake for 25–30 mins. Cool and frost.

Red Velvet Cake

Lemon Drizzle Cake

  • 1½ cups self-rising flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • ½ cup powdered sugar (for drizzle)
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Cream butter and sugar, then mix in eggs and lemon zest.
  2. Fold in flour. Pour into pan and bake 30–35 mins.
  3. Mix lemon juice with powdered sugar and drizzle over warm cake.

🎓 Luxe Bites Baking Tips & Hacks

  • 🍰 Always preheat your oven for accurate temperature.
  • 🧈 Bring butter and eggs to room temperature for a smooth batter.
  • 🥄 Measure ingredients using proper measuring tools for precision.
  • 🔁 Don’t overmix batter; mix until just combined for soft texture.
  • 🎂 Test cakes with a toothpick — if it comes out clean, it’s done.
  • ❄ Let cakes cool before removing from pans to avoid breaking.
  • 🌈 Add a teaspoon of vinegar or lemon juice to buttermilk for fluffiness.
  • 🧁 Freeze cakes before frosting for clean, smooth finishes.
  • 🎨 Use food coloring gels instead of liquids for vibrant colors.
  • 👩‍🍳 Clean as you go — makes baking more enjoyable!
